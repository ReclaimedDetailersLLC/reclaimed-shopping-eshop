Who We Are

Built by Enthusiasts,
for Enthusiasts

Reclaimed Group started in a garage with a single mission: to restore vehicles to their best possible condition using products that actually work. We got tired of buying hyped-up products with flashy packaging that underdelivered — so we tested dozens of formulas, coating technologies, and detailing systems until we found the ones that earned a spot in our lineup.

Today, every product we carry has been personally vetted on real vehicles in real conditions. If it didn't pass our test, it didn't make the cut. Simple as that.

We're more than a supply company — we're a resource. Have a question about paint correction? Not sure which coating is right for your situation? Reach out. We're a small team and we take pride in giving real answers, not scripted sales pitches.
