This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Reclaimed Group LLC ("we," "us," or "our") uses them on our website, and how you can control or disable them. By using our website and accepting our cookie notice, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, and to provide information to the owners of a site.

Cookies can be "persistent" (remaining on your device for a set period or until you delete them) or "session-based" (deleted when you close your browser). They can also be "first-party" (set by the website you are visiting) or "third-party" (set by a domain different from the website you are visiting).

2. What Cookies Do We Use?

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core features such as remembering your cookie consent choice and preserving your shopping cart contents across pages. These cookies cannot be disabled without significantly affecting how the website functions.

  • rc_cookies — Stores your cookie consent preference. Expires after 1 year.
  • rc_cart — Stores your shopping cart items in your browser's local storage so your cart persists between visits. This is stored locally on your device, not transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

If you accept all cookies, we may use analytics tools to help us understand how visitors use our website. This information is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify you personally. Analytics cookies may track:

  • Pages you visit and how long you spend on them
  • How you arrived at our website (e.g., search engine, direct link)
  • Browser and device type
  • General geographic region

You may opt out of analytics cookies by selecting "Essential Only" on our cookie consent banner, or by adjusting your browser settings (see Section 4 below).

2.3 Preference Cookies (Optional)

These cookies remember your preferences to enhance your experience on future visits, such as your last-used filter or sort settings in the shop. These are stored in local browser storage and not transmitted to any third party.

2.4 Cookies We Do NOT Use

We do not use cookies for:

  • Advertising or retargeting
  • Selling data to third parties
  • Cross-site tracking for commercial purposes
  • Payment or financial data collection (we do not process payments online)

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses Google Fonts (loaded from fonts.googleapis.com) to display custom typography. Google may set cookies or collect technical information when fonts are loaded. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy for more information. Font loading does not involve any personal data you submit on our forms.

Our website may contain links to third-party services or platforms (e.g., social media). These third parties may set their own cookies when you interact with their content. We have no control over third-party cookies and recommend reviewing their respective policies.

4. Managing and Disabling Cookies

You have the right to accept or decline non-essential cookies. When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie consent banner with the option to:

  • Accept All — enables all cookies including optional analytics and preference cookies.
  • Essential Only — only strictly necessary cookies are used; analytics are disabled.

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, and be notified when new cookies are set. Please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website (e.g., your cart contents may not be saved).

Browser-specific instructions:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

5. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to store your shopping cart data (via the rc_cart key) and your cookie consent preference (rc_cookies key). Local storage data is stored on your device only and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear local storage through your browser's developer tools or by clearing your browser's "site data" for our website.
