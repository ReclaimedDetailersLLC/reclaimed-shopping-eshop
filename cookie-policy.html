Reclaimed ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, what types we use, and how you can control them. By clicking "Accept All" on our cookie banner, you consent to the use of all cookies described below. If you choose "Essential Only," only strictly necessary cookies will be used.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences or items in your cart. Cookies do not contain your personal information directly, but information we store separately may be linked to information obtained from cookies.

We also use localStorage — a browser storage mechanism similar in function to cookies — to store your shopping cart items and cookie consent preference locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly submit a form.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • rc_cookies — Stores your cookie consent preference (localStorage). Required to remember your choice.
  • rc_cart — Stores your shopping cart items locally (localStorage). Required for the cart to function between page loads.

These are essential for the site to work and are always active, regardless of your consent choice.

2.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

If you have accepted all cookies, we may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. Information collected includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • How you arrived at our site (referring source)
  • General geographic region (country/state level — not precise location)
  • Device and browser type

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We use it to improve the site experience — nothing more. We do not sell this data to third parties.

2.3 Cookies We Do Not Use

  • We do not use advertising or retargeting cookies.
  • We do not use social media tracking pixels.
  • We do not use any cookies that collect your payment information (we don't process payments online).

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses Google Fonts (loaded from fonts.googleapis.com) to display custom typography. Google may set cookies or collect technical information when fonts are loaded. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy for more information. Font loading does not involve any personal data you submit on our forms.

Our website may contain links to third-party services or platforms (e.g., social media). These third parties may set their own cookies when you interact with their content. We have no control over third-party cookies and recommend reviewing their respective policies.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit our site, a cookie consent banner will appear giving you the choice to accept all cookies or essential-only cookies. Your preference is saved in localStorage under the key rc_cookies.

To change your preference, clear your browser's localStorage for this site (available in your browser's developer tools or site settings), then reload the page. The consent banner will reappear.

Browser-specific instructions for managing cookies:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Note that disabling all cookies may affect the functionality of some website features, including the shopping cart.
